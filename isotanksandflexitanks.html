ISO Tanks and Flexi Tanks

  • ISO Tanks and Flexi Tanks

ISO tanks and flexi tanks are innovative solutions in the world of logistics, designed to transport liquids in a safe, efficient, and cost-effective manner across different modes of transportation.

ISO Tanks:

ISO tanks are durable, cylindrical containers built to international standards (ISO standards) for the transportation of liquids, including chemicals, food-grade products, and hazardous materials. These tanks are constructed to withstand varying pressures and are compatible with multiple modes of transportation like ships, trains, and trucks.

Key Aspects of ISO Tanks:

Robust Construction: ISO tanks are made from high-grade stainless steel, offering durability and resistance to corrosion, ensuring the safe transport of liquids.

Versatility: These tanks come in various sizes and capacities, providing flexibility to transport different quantities of liquids according to specific shipment requirements.

Intermodal Compatibility: ISO tanks are designed to be easily transferred between different modes of transport without the need for unloading and reloading, allowing for a seamless journey across various transportation networks.

Flexi Tanks:

Flexi tanks are flexible, collapsible containers that convert standard transportation containers (such as 20-foot shipping containers) into efficient bulk liquid transport units. They are typically made from multiple layers of specialized materials designed to hold a wide range of non-hazardous liquids.

Key Aspects of Flexi Tanks:

Space Optimization: Flexi tanks maximize space within standard shipping containers, allowing for the transport of larger liquid volumes compared to traditional drums or barrels.

Cost-Efficiency: They offer a cost-effective alternative for transporting non-hazardous liquids in bulk, reducing transportation costs compared to traditional tank containers.

Environmental Benefits: Flexi tanks reduce the need for cleaning and maintenance associated with reusable containers, minimizing the environmental impact of liquid transportation.

Both ISO tanks and flexi tanks offer efficient and secure ways to transport liquids, catering to different needs and requirements. They provide safe containment, easy handling, and cost-effective solutions for transporting various liquid commodities across domestic and international borders.
